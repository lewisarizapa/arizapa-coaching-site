FAQs

Do I need to be local to Canberra?

No – online coaching is fully remote. All you need is a gym membership and a willingness to execute.

Is there a lock-in contract?

No fixed contract, but I recommend committing to at least 12 weeks. Cancellations require 7 days’ notice so I can organise spots.

Do you write meal plans?

I focus on calorie and macro targets with food guidance and examples so you can build a sustainable way of eating that fits your lifestyle.

How are payments handled?

Payments are processed weekly, fortnightly or monthly via bank transfer or Stripe. Details are confirmed once your application is accepted.
